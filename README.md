## APOD app
Explore the Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) and discover the wonders of the universe.

## Description
This app retrieves and shows Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) from [NASA APOD API](https://api.nasa.gov).

## Get starting
Choose a date and you get pictures/video shown on your screen.
- If you choose a day between 1995-6-16 and current day, you'll get 4 pictures/videos of 4 days beginning at the day you choose.
- If you choose a day in future (after current day), you get a picture/video of a random day.
For each picture/video, you can Open/Close the short explaination by astronomers to have more insight about it.
